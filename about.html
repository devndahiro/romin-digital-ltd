Our Journey

Founded in 2018, Romin Digital emerged from a shared vision to revolutionize Africa's digital landscape through cutting-edge technology solutions and transformative training programs.

What started as a small web development startup in Kigali, Rwanda, has evolved into a pan-African digital powerhouse, delivering exceptional results for clients across 4 countries and counting.

Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and client success has driven us to expand our services, grow our team, and continuously adapt to the ever-changing technology landscape.

2018 - Foundation

Started as a web development studio in Kigali

2020 - Expansion

Added mobile app development and IT training

2022 - Growth

Expanded to serve clients across East Africa

2025 - Innovation

Leading digital transformation across Africa
